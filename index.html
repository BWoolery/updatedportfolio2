About Me

Thanks for taking the time to check out my bio! After spending a few years helping my husband establish a dental practice, I am eager to pursue a career I am passionate about.



Born in 1985 in Media, Pennsylvania, I spent my childhood in the suburbs outside Philadelphia. After completing my bachelors degree at Penn State University, I taught high school Social Studies for 6 years before moving to Ocean City, New Jersey. I life long learner, I have a masters degree in education and hope to one day use the skills I acquire during bootcamp to work for an educational company.
